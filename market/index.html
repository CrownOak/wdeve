Price
Accepts Name, Name 1000, Name x50, 50 x Name, or tab-pasted from in game. to buy uses the robust cheap sell price (what you pay); to sell uses the top buy order (what you get). Cheapest hub is highlighted. Unknown items are struck. Region prices, live, never logged.
Paste a multibuy and hit Price it.
Resolves each name to its type via ESI, then pulls live region market aggregates for all five hubs (Jita, Amarr, Dodixie, Rens, Hek) from Fuzzwork. Each cell is the line cost (quantity times the robust price), each column totals its hub, and the cheapest hub is highlighted. to buy prices from sell orders using the robust cheapest 5% (so one mispriced order cannot fake a deal); to sell prices into the best buy orders. An item with no order on that side at a hub shows n/a and is left out of that hub total only. Names we cannot find stay in the list, struck, and count for nothing. This is order book data, not a promise: prices move, and the gank on the way home is still your problem.
